Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Introduction

At PureComfort, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website or services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Booking information (e.g., dates of stay, room preferences)
  • Payment information (e.g., credit card details)
  • Website usage data (e.g., IP address, browser type)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and manage your bookings
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and website
  • Send promotional offers and updates (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties unless we provide you with advance notice or it is necessary to fulfill our services to you.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Request erasure of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
